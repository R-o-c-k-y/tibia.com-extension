Reckless

Guild Information
One does not simply walk through the path of recklessness - his heart must be steadily blazing, willing to bound with the brotherhood; then, and only then, he will fully become Reckless. Friends only.

The guild was founded on Wintera on Sep 12 2014.
It is currently active.
Guild is opened for applications.
Their home on Wintera is Halls of Serenity. The rent is paid until Jan 16 2015.
